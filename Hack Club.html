Hack Club

Hack Club is a network of many of the most talented teenage programmers in the world, and I'm honored to work alongside the founder on the board, and as Hack Club's COO, focusing on strategy, fundraising, communications and gender equity.

Hack Club is building a new generation of hackers with the mindset, network and hard technical skills to start solving some of our biggest challenges. The community is diverse, welcoming and accessible to all.

Hack Club has 4 parts:

  • Student-led computer science clubs
  • Slack community, where students hang out, learn from each other, collaborate on projects and have fun
  • Workshops & Events, where students have live chats with Elon Musk, Tom Preston Werner, Limor Fried and take intro-workshops to Python, CSS and React JS
  • Hack Club Bank: we are fiscal sponsor for hundreds of student-led organizations, and our community has done more than $2m in transactions in the last 2 years.
At the heart of Hack Club is the projects students build. Check out some of the projects young people are making and shipping at Hack Club.

Hack Club is a non-profit, and thanks to generous donors we are creating a community for thousands of young people on their journey to becoming kind, curious and optimistic leaders who master technology to build a better future.
