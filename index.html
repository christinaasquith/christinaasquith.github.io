Christina Asquith

Hi there! My name is Christina, and I'm Chief Operating Officer and board member of Hack Club, a global tech non-profit that is creating a new generation of coders and innovators.

I'm also founder of The Fuller Project, a global nonprofit journalism organization that investigates issues impacting women. For 7 years, I grew Fuller Project, leading our investigative journalism as editor-in-chief, raising the first $5million in donations, and building out all our media partnerships, including with TIME Magazine, for whom Fuller Project had the cover story in 2019.

My career includes years reporting for The New York Times, Economist, The Philadelphia Inquirer and Public Radio International, and editing pieces for The New Yorker, The Atlantic and Washington Post. My 2 nonfiction books are on women in the Iraq war, and as my year as a 6th grade teacher in North Philadelphia. I've lived in Baghdad, Istanbul, London and Santiago, Chile. I've been interviewed on NPR, BBC, ABC News, Al Jazeera and PRI's The World.
